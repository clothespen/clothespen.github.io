Clothes Pen

Our Mission

We may sell clothes, but we are all about community. Whether through local projects like our free-to-the-public laundromat, fundraising drives like our Blankets or Bust and Back-to-School Backpack programs, or through offering our affordable prices on clothing items, we are an organization dedicated to providing for whatever our community's needs may be. We also want to make sure that what you give is what you get, which is why we also provide you with how our charity funds are being used each year. Generosity and transparency for our community. That's Clothes Pen.
