Back-to-School Backpack Drive

50%

Date: 8/4/2018
Time: 8am - 12pm

The Back-to-School Backpack Drive is our way of helping to reduce local parents' school supplies bill, hopefully reducing the stress, too. Although we're primarily collecting backpacks, (typically a big-ticket item for parents) we happily accept school supplies of all kinds and deliver it all to local schools, who then give to children in need.

Volunteers for this event will man the donation booths setup across the area and deliver all donations collected throughout the day to our headquarters. We'll take care of getting these school supplies to the children who need them. 5 More Volunteers Needed!
