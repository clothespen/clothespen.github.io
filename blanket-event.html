Blankets or Bust Blanket Drive

75%

Date: 10/10/2018
Time: 12pm - 5pm

The Blankets or Bust Blanket Drive helps keep our community's homeless and less fortunate members warm during the winter. Collecting blankets that aren't being used anymore, or even brand-new ones, we give all donations to our local homeless shelters and foster care centers.

Volunteers for this event will man the various donation tables setup across the area and deliver all donations collected throughout the day to our headquarters. We'll take care of sorting and getting these blankets to the people who need them. 2 More Volunteers Needed!
